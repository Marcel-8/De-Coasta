Our Journey So Far

The name "De'Costa" means "of the coast," a name deeply rooted in the history of exploration and trade that shaped Ghana.

The Genesis of De'Costa Travel

Our journey began with a simple yet profound passion: to share the profound beauty and untold stories of our homeland, from the sun-drenched beaches of the Atlantic to the ancient kingdoms of the interior. We believe that true travel is about immersion, not just sightseeing.

Founded by a family with generations of history tied to Ghana's coastal towns, De'Costa Travel was born from a desire to create authentic, immersive, and unforgettable travel experiences. We are not just a tour company; we are storytellers, guides, and your trusted partners in adventure, committed to showcasing Ghana's rich heritage and warm hospitality.
